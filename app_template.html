📱 Bewiched App — Usage Dashboard

Prepared for VickyApp project · v1 draft
How much of Bewiched trade goes through the app, over time and vs the wider market. Source: live BigQuery POS.
What counts as an "app" transaction? A till order with a customer name attached (a registered app/loyalty account — these all carry an email). This is the agreed definition for this draft. Caveat for Matt: it captures every order tied to a known customer — that includes in-store loyalty scans, not only "order-ahead via the app". So treat it as app/loyalty-identified transactions (an upper bound on pure mobile order-ahead). It is the same basis as Greggs' published "app scanned" loyalty metric, but not strictly like-for-like with chains that quote "mobile order & pay".

App share by store — quarter to date (1 Apr – 14 Jun 2026)
Big spread across the estate — the drive-thrus & community sites (Burton, Glenvale, Balsall, Northampton DT) run 50–60%+, while transient/commuter sites (Wellingborough Train Station, Northampton high street, Rushden Lakes) sit nearer 18–27%. A clear adoption-gap map for the app project.
Market comparison — app / mobile / loyalty share of transactions
External published figures — not strictly like-for-like. Each chain measures a slightly different thing: Bewiched = app/loyalty-identified transactions (named customer); Greggs = loyalty app "scanned" share; Starbucks = "Mobile Order & Pay" share (US); Pret = digital (Club Pret) transaction share. They're a directional benchmark, not an exact comparison. Costa quotes ">70% of Costa Club transactions via app" — that's a share of loyalty, not of total transactions, so it's excluded from the bar (shown here as a note). Caffè Nero publishes app users (3m+) but no clean transaction-share figure.
