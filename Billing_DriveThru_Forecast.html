How F1 works. Every store is audited unannounced through the week. Qualifying (Mon–Thu) is a lighter check — queue speed plus hospitality (Hello, Goodbye, How-Are-You, Working the Queue) — and sets the grid for the weekend. Race (Fri–Sun) is the full audit (tables to brand, cleared within 3 mins, section/break-cover plan, visible team, dishwasher area). Lower queue seconds and higher championship points are better. Billing has only just entered the championship — two Race audits in, no Qualifying yet.
→Focus this week: P17 and queue 535s→178s in a week — strong direction; keep the routine.
