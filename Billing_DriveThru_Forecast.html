Sales by category — holiday-mix forecast
CategoryCurrent mixHoliday tiltCapture %
Mix holds through the holidays. In the mature drive-thru/destination stores last summer the split barely moved from term into the school holidays — cold drinks + milkshakes were ~37% of sales in term vs ~35% in holidays (milkshakes flat, cold easing ~2pp as the early-summer heat passed). The cold-drink peak is weather-led (Jun–Jul), not the school calendar; total volume rose ~5%. So £ grows across all categories with only a small holiday tilt. Capture % = the share of transactions that include the category (attach rate, recent weeks) — a truer read of penetration than £.
⚠️ Wastage — high & volatile for a drive-thru
Billing's wastage is running hot — 4.7% → 8.3% → 3.7% across its first three weeks (avg 5.6%), well above mature drive-thrus (NDT 0.9%, Glenvale 2.8%). On its volume, reaching a 3% target is worth ~£4,650 of retail waste over the quarter (~£2,100 at cost) — the biggest wastage prize of the new sites. The sandwich/toastie range is the issue.
