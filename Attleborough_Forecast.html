How F1 works. Every store is audited unannounced through the week. Qualifying (Mon–Thu) is a lighter check — queue speed plus hospitality (Hello, Goodbye, How-Are-You, Working the Queue) — and sets the grid for the weekend. Race (Fri–Sun) is the full audit (adds tables to brand, cleared within 3 mins, section/break-cover plan, visible team, dishwasher area) with the start handicapped by the Qualifying result. Lower queue/lap seconds and higher championship points are better.
→Focus this week: P2 podium two weekends running — bank it; fix the Mon door welcome (Hello 0% on 1 Jun).
